Knobo: Knowlege Onboarding Assistant for Larger Engineering Teams

Problem

  • No one engineer has the knowledge of the entire system architecture.
  • System is generally broken down into different capabilities.
  • Senior engineers are the Point of Contacts (PoCs) for knowledge with each capability.

A new engineer who needs to understand the system OR a team leader who needs answers for specific questions needs to set up 1:1 time with the specific PoC for knowledge sync.

This leads to:

  • Time lost waiting for sync meetings to answer basic questions.
  • Time lost to sometimes contacting multiple PoCs to get to complete answer.
  • Time wasted for PoCs having to answer repetitive questions.

Solution

Ask Knobo (Knowledge Onboarding Assistant) instead. Just upload documentation or wikis and it will answer the questions at your own time without bothering the PoC.

Advantages:

  • Time saved in getting answer needed.
    • For new engineers, this could be faster onboarding with better understanding.
    • For leaders, time can be even more critical.
  • Time saved for PoCs.
  • PoCs are responsible for maintaining accurate documentation.
  • Knowledge is transparently maintained across the team.
  • Developer productivity is higher.
